About the Conclave

Welcome to the 2nd edition of IEEE Bangalore Technology Conclave (BTC) on the theme Clean Technologies for a Sustainable World.

IEEE BTC 2021 is a flagship event of IEEE Bangalore Section, organized in collaboration with its two affinity groups - Women in Engineering (WiE) and Young Professionals (YP). It is held annually as part of the Industry Relations program of the Section; where technology leaders, innovators, entrepreneurs, and thought provokers share their passion in addressing impactful problems, and vision on futuristic technologies that will/may disrupt/impact our way of working and living.

This edition of IEEE BTC will be based on the theme of CleanTech; with a specific focus on:

  1. sustainable transport and renewable energy storage;
  2. ICT for clean technologies;
  3. clean production and clean materials; and
  4. sustainable agriculture, from farm to fork.

Subject matter experts will share their views on some of the following points:

  • What are the CleanTech challenges in India and Globally?
  • What are the top problems confronting this sector?
  • What are the forthcoming trends in this sector?
  • What are the opportunities that are likely to arise as a consequence of these trends, and their effect on present day's ground situation
  • What are the kind of solutions and measures (technical/non-technical) that are in place to address such problems, and their effectiveness level?
  • What is the role of citizens, industry, government and its agencies, academia in mitigating this problem ?
  • Should we be 'bullish' or 'bearish' about the future prospects of this sector?
