Shivkumar Kalyanaraman

CTO, Energy & Mobility, Microsoft India

* CTO, Energy & Mobility, Microsoft India (Azure Global cloud business). Building a global Industry Platform-as-a-service (IPAAS) business for Microsoft's Azure, including end-to-end technical, business strategy, execution & strategic partnership responsibilities. Scope: clean energy (solar, wind, storage), automotive / mobility / transportation (connected, e-mobility, TaaS), and cross-domain industry plays.

* Previously Executive GM in GE Power Conversion: built new Line of Business (LOB) P&L ground up (across product, sales, strategy/business dev) in e-Mobility and Commercial & Industrial (C&I) Solar.

* Visionary technology and business executive with first-principles thinking, deep technology skills, strategic insight, client/business development experience and delivered business impact across USA, Europe, India, Asia Pacific (Australia, Brunei, Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, Malaysia) & Middle East.

* Developed and led industry solutions teams involving transformative digital technologies: Artificial Intelligence (AI), machine learning/deep learning, Internet of Things (IoT), blockchain, mobile & cloud computing, telecom and wireless / networking.

* Developed & delivered industry solutions / platforms in areas such as energy & utilities (eg: renewables/solar/wind, storage, smart grids), natural resources (agri-business, mining, water, oil & gas), transportation (eg: electric vehicles (EV) & shared transportation), with deep strategic & financial business insights (eg: see linkedin articles).

* Highly eminent/visible technologist. I leverage my education / academic past (as tenured full professor in RPI, USA (1997-2007)) and extensive networks to attract, hire and grow talent, with a special focus on diversity.

* I develop and care about people: lead by inspiration, learning & encourage bold first-principles based thinking supported by frugal, relentless execution.

* 200+ peer reviewed technical papers; 35+ patents granted (50+ filed), IBM Master Inventor awardee (2017).

Recognition: Distinguished Alumni Award for Career Achievement (Ohio State University, 2021), Distinguished Alumnus Award (IIT Madras, 2021), IEEE Fellow (2010, contributions to network traffic management), ACM Distinguished Scientist (2010-11), Indian National Academy of Engineering (INAE) Fellow (2015), MIT Technology Review TR 100 (1999), IBM Master Inventor (2017).
