Krupa Rasane

Organizing Committee Member, WiE Global Summit

Dr. Krupa Rasane is HoD of Jain College of Engineering, Belgavi. Her areas of interests are Embedded Systems Design, ASIC Design and VLSI Design. She is an IEEE Senior Member, WIE Execom member Bangolore section and reviewer of International Conference. Organized as Convener of 3 International Conferences. Subject Expert for EDUSAT (VTU e-learning) for subject Signals and Systems. Branch Counsellor IEEE Student Branch. Have been the member of the Board of Education (BOE) for the UG and PG, Exam VTU Scrutiny Committee, Paper Setting and Evaluation both at UG/PG/PhD level at VTU, Belgaum. Traveled abroad to countries like Singapore and Malaysia and presented PhD papers. Subject Domains are Embedded Systems, ASIC Design, VLSI Algorithms, WSN architecture, Image Compression
