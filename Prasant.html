Organizing committee

Prasant Misra

Treasurer, IEEE Bangalore Section

Mentor - Professional Activities Committee & Industry Committee

TCS Research

Prasant is a Scientist in TCS Research and Innovation at TATA Consultancy Services Ltd., Bangalore. He performs research in computational sensing with a focus on cyber-physical systems. He also has a background in middleware for wireless sensor networks and low-power networked embedded systems.

Prasant has many years of experience in scientific and industrial research. His scientific excellence has been recognized and felicitated by a number of honors and awards, of which it is noteworthy to mention the: MIT TR35 – India “Top 10 Innovators under 35 in India” and TCS “Exemplary Contribution Award” in 2017, ERCIM “Alain Bensoussan” and EC “Marie Curie” Fellowship in 2012, and the Australian Government’s AusAID “Australian Leadership Awards” in 2008.

Prasant is an active volunteer. He has served on the organizing and technical committee of a number of international conferences/events. He has represented TCS and India in many national and international standardization forums on Smart Cities. He is an active speaker and thought provoker on the subject of IoT and its various nuances. He is a senior member of IEEE and ACM, member of the executive committee of IEEE Bangalore Section, and an Associate Technical Editor of IEEE Communication Magazine. His volunteering excellence and impact has been felicitated by the: IEEE Bangalore Section “Outstanding Volunteer Award for the year 2017”.

Prasant completed his postdoctoral fellowship from the Swedish Institute of Computer Science, Stockholm (2013), and received his Ph.D. from the University of New South Wales, Sydney (2012); both in the field of computer science and engineering.
