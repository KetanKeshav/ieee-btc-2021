Vidya Hungud

Executive Director, JPMorgan Chase & Co.

Passionate technology and customer obsessed leader with extensive experience in creating vision, strategising and building high-throughput E-commerce and enterprise cloud applications, resulting in double digit growth both in web and mobile.

Worked in US and India at startup, medium and large tech companies on Saas, IaaS, PaaS products with Master’s Degree in Computer Engineering, San Jose, CA.

Fosters innovation culture to create an awesome customer experience and brings positive impact to the business.

Successful in driving engineering excellence, building high performing global teams, nurturing, channelizing great talent and coaching teams to break down abstract business needs into actionable plans.
