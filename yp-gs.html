About the Summit

IEEE Young Professionals Bangalore in association with IEEE Bangalore Section presents to you “Young Professionals Global Summit 2020”. A two-day virtual experience featuring young achievers across diverse domains of technology and entrepreneurship. We have keynotes, workshops, and panel discussions in topics such as 5G, V2X, IoT, computing, semiconductors,

machine learning, artificial intelligence, and space and satellite technologies. Below entails the detailed schedule of the event. Gear up and register to experience and take part in the most insightful, thought-provoking, and technically rich summit this November!
