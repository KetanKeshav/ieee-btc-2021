About the Summit

IEEE Young Professionals Affinity Group Bangalore in association with IEEE Bangalore Section is excited to host “Young Professionals Global Summit 2021” at IEEE Bangalore Technology Conclave (BTC), 2021. Featuring young achievers in domains of technology and entrepreneurship, we plan to enthral you with a two-day virtual experience with TWO major attractions. On one side, the YP Global Summit 2021 is going to have illuminating technical talks delivered by young tech-Leaders from the industry.

Following the overarching theme of 'Clean Technologies for a Sustainable World', the technical talks will cater topics like 5G, V2X, IoT, computing, semiconductors, machine learning, artificial intelligence, space and satellite technologies.The second mega-event will be a Fireside Chat with the IEEE Bangalore TechICONS, where the experiences and deliberations of the TechICONs would be like guidance to the Young Professionals in their career.
